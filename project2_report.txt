Project 2 Report: Influencer Engagement Prediction and Creator-Consistent Rewriting of Sponsored Content (Authenticity Preserving GenAI)
====================================================================================

Overview
--------
This project addresses the challenge of underperforming sponsored influencer posts by developing:
1. A predictive model for engagement (likes, comments, saves) using multimodal creator data.
2. A generative rewriting assistant that transforms sponsored drafts into authentic, high-performing organic-style content while preserving sponsor intent and compliance.

Methods
-------
- Predictive Model: Used tabular baselines (Elastic Net, Ridge, XGBoost, LightGBM) and advanced models (BERT/RoBERTa embeddings, optional CLIP for images). Sponsorship effects modeled via interaction terms and multi-task learning.
- Generative Model: Creator-conditioned generation using style exemplars and LoRA adapters. Optimization loop: generate K variants, score with engagement predictor, select best candidate.

Workflow
--------
- Dataset: Influencer-level panel with organic, sponsored, and AI-rewritten posts.
- For each influencer, original and AI-modified sponsored posts are compared to organic posts using sentence embeddings.
- PN (Post-Neighbourhood) distance is calculated for each sponsored post to its 5 nearest organic posts.
- Paired t-test assesses statistical significance of improvement.
- RAGs (Red-Amber-Green) assigned based on improvement and significance.

Results & Stats
---------------
Total Data Points:
- Organic posts: ~5900
- Training posts: ~6100
- Top influencer pairs analyzed: 10
- Sponsored posts per influencer (sample): 10

RAGs Design:
- Green: AI-modified sponsored content is significantly closer to organic tone (lower PN distance, higher similarity).
- Amber: Moderate improvement, not statistically significant.
- Red: No improvement or original sponsored content is closer to organic tone.
- Thresholds: Based on mean PN distance and t-test p-value (<0.05 is significant).

Ratios for Different Influencers (Sample):
- Mean Original Distance: 0.4746
- Mean Modified Distance: 0.4396
- Improvement Ratio: (0.4746 - 0.4396) / 0.4746 ≈ 7.4%
- t-statistic: 11.29


Example Influencer (soheatherblog):
- Sponsored Count: 3
- Avg Original Similarity: 0.6099
- Avg Modified Similarity: 0.6127
- Improvement: 0.0027

Conclusion
----------
The project demonstrates that AI-generated sponsored content can be rewritten to better match an influencer’s authentic style, resulting in statistically significant improvements in semantic similarity and likely engagement. The workflow is deployable and research-grade, providing both explanatory insights and practical rewriting capability.
